## Compliance & Best Practices

### Receipt Requirements
**Must Include**:
- Date of purchase (after program completion)
- Vendor name and address
- Itemized list of goods/services
- Total amount paid
- Payment method
- Clear business purpose

### Documentation Tips
1. **Keep Original Receipts**: Digital copies acceptable
2. **Business Purpose**: Always include clear business justification
3. **Timing**: All receipts must be from after program completion
4. **Categories**: Organize receipts by business function
5. **Backup**: Keep copies of all documentation

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid
- Personal expenses mixed with business expenses
- Receipts from before program completion
- Unclear business purpose
- Missing vendor information
- Incomplete documentation
