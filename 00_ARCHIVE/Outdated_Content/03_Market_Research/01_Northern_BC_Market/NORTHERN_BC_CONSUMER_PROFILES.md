# Northern BC Market Research & Consumer Profiles

## Northern BC Market Overview

### Population Distribution (Total: ~360,000 residents)
- **Fraser-Fort George** (Prince George): ~96,167 residents
- **Cariboo** (Williams Lake, Quesnel): ~64,809 residents  
- **Peace River** (Fort St. John, Dawson Creek): ~62,945 residents
- **Kitimat-Stikine** (Terrace, Kitimat): ~39,447 residents
- **Bulkley-Nechako** (Smithers, Houston): ~39,304 residents
- **Skeena-Queen Charlotte** (Prince Rupert): ~19,958 residents
- **Northern Rockies** (Fort Nelson): ~5,924 residents

### Key Industries
- **Forestry**: Logging, sawmills, pulp mills
- **Mining**: Coal, gold, copper, natural gas
- **Energy**: Hydroelectric, oil & gas, LNG
- **Tourism**: Outdoor recreation, Indigenous tourism
- **Healthcare**: Regional hospitals and clinics
- **Education**: Universities, colleges, schools
- **Transportation**: Rail, trucking, ports
- **Agriculture**: Ranching, farming, food processing

### Digital Engagement
- **Social Media Active**: 360,000+ users
- **High Digital Connectivity**: Strong online presence
- **Community-Focused**: Local business preference
- **Mobile-First**: Rural connectivity patterns
