# Service Offerings Strategy

## Service Categories Overview

### 01_Rabbits (Quick Wins)
**Target**: Small businesses, startups, individual entrepreneurs  
**Timeline**: 1-4 weeks  
**Investment**: $500-$2,000  
**Focus**: Immediate impact, low complexity, high ROI

#### Rabbit Services
- **AI Tool Setup**: ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini implementation
- **Process Automation**: Simple workflow automation
- **Content Generation**: AI-powered content creation
- **Data Analysis**: Basic business intelligence
- **Customer Service**: AI chatbot implementation
- **Social Media**: AI-assisted social media management

### 02_Deer (Medium Projects)
**Target**: Growing businesses, established SMEs  
**Timeline**: 1-3 months  
**Investment**: $2,000-$10,000  
**Focus**: Strategic implementation, measurable outcomes

#### Deer Services
- **AI Strategy Development**: Comprehensive AI roadmap
- **Custom AI Solutions**: Tailored AI implementations
- **Process Optimization**: End-to-end workflow improvement
- **Data Integration**: Multi-system data connectivity
- **Predictive Analytics**: Business forecasting and insights
- **AI Training**: Staff education and upskilling

### 03_Elephants (Enterprise Solutions)
**Target**: Large businesses, corporations, government  
**Timeline**: 3-12 months  
**Investment**: $10,000-$100,000+  
**Focus**: Transformational change, competitive advantage

#### Elephant Services
- **AI Transformation**: Complete business transformation
- **Custom AI Platforms**: Proprietary AI solutions
- **Advanced Analytics**: Complex data science projects
- **AI Infrastructure**: Enterprise-grade AI systems
- **Strategic Consulting**: C-suite AI advisory
- **AI Governance**: Risk management and compliance

### 04_AI_Products (Product Development)
**Target**: All business sizes  
**Timeline**: Ongoing  
**Investment**: Variable  
**Focus**: Scalable solutions, recurring revenue

#### AI Products
- **Terrace AI**: Local business intelligence platform
- **Voice Recorder AI**: Meeting transcription and analysis
- **AI Dashboard**: Business performance monitoring
- **AI Templates**: Ready-to-use AI solutions
- **AI Training Courses**: Educational programs
- **AI Consulting Packages**: Standardized service offerings

### 05_Physical_Products (Hardware Solutions)
**Target**: Businesses requiring hardware integration  
**Timeline**: 3-6 months  
**Investment**: $5,000-$50,000  
**Focus**: Hardware-software integration, IoT solutions

#### Physical Products
- **AI Kiosks**: Interactive customer service terminals
- **Smart Sensors**: IoT data collection devices
- **AI Cameras**: Computer vision solutions
- **Voice Devices**: AI-powered audio systems
- **Data Centers**: Local AI processing infrastructure
- **Hardware Integration**: Existing system enhancement
