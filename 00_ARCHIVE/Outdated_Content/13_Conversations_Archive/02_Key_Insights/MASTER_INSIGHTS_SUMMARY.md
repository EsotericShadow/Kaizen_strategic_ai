# Kaizen Strategic AI - Master Insights Summary

## Core Business Philosophy
- **Continuous Improvement**: "Work less, achieve more" - the essence of Kaizen
- **Human-First AI**: Technology should serve people, not replace them
- **Northern Roots**: Built for the North, by the North - understanding local realities
- **Practical Over Hype**: Real tools that work, not trendy buzzwords

## Brand Evolution Journey
### From Evergreen Web Solutions → Kaizen Strategic AI
- **Original Identity**: Local web design with automation
- **New Identity**: Strategic AI consultancy with digital infrastructure focus
- **Key Insight**: Websites are the entry point, AI/automation is the value
- **Positioning**: "Digital strategy for the real world"

## Mission & Vision (Finalized)
### Mission Statement
"Evergreen Web Solutions exists to move the North forward. We build real tools — not trends — that help local businesses work smarter, grow faster, and stay independent in a world that's getting automated whether we like it or not. We believe technology should serve people, not replace them. Our mission is to bridge that gap — bringing small-town grit and modern systems together so the next generation of Northern businesses can compete on a global level without losing what makes them real."

### Vision Statement
"We see a North where every business feels at home in the digital world — confident in their systems, clear on their direction, and proud of what they've built. Our vision is simple: make technology feel human again. When people understand the tools behind their business, they stop fearing change and start leading it — and that's how the North moves forward."

## Value Proposition
"We make technology make sense. Evergreen helps Northern businesses understand and control their digital systems — no jargon, no guesswork, just results that fit the way you actually work."

- **Profile**: 1-5 employees, $100K-$500K revenue
- **Budget**: $2K-$5K per project
- **Offer**: Starter Systems Package (website + basic automation)
- **Timeline**: 2-4 weeks

### Deer (Core Clients) - PRIMARY FOCUS
- **Profile**: 5-50 employees, $500K-$5M revenue

## Market Research Key Findings
### Northern BC Market Reality
- **Terrace Population**: 12,017 (2021)
- **Economic Region**: North Coast & Nechako (~69,700 population)
- **Key Industries**: Manufacturing, forestry, mining, fishing, tourism
- **Tourism**: ~$1B spending, ~920 businesses, 8,400 employees

### AI Adoption Trends
- **Canadian SMBs**: 71% actively using AI, 70% report improved efficiency
- **Highest Adoption**: Information/cultural (35.6%), professional services (31.7%)
- **Market Timing**: Early-mover advantage in Northern BC

### Funding Opportunities
- **BC Employer Training Grant**: 80% reimbursement for training costs
- **Northern Development Initiative Trust**: Multiple business programs
- **CDAP**: Ended Feb 2024 (need to de-risk with clear ROI)
