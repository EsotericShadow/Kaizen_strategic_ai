## Business Evolution Analysis

### The Rebrand Rationale
**From**: Evergreen Web Solutions (web development focus)
**To**: Kaizen Strategic AI (AI strategy and implementation)

**Strategic Shift**: Moving upstream in the value chain from "web developer" to "AI strategist"

### Key Insights from Conversations

#### 1. Competitive Advantage Realization
- **Previous competitors** (web designers, dev shops, IT providers) can now become **clients**
- **No direct AI competition** in Northern BC region
- **Market positioning**: From service provider to regional thought leader

#### 2. Market Awareness Reality Check
- **Current event attendance**: Only 14 people (October 2025)
- **Market penetration**: Still early in awareness curve
- **Target**: Need to reach more business owners and decision makers
- **Population**: 360,000+ in Northern BC region

#### 3. Professional Legitimacy Validation
**AI Strategy Consulting is a legitimate profession**:
- Major consultancies (Accenture, Deloitte, PwC) have AI strategy divisions
- Gap exists between IT providers and AI specialists
- Market needs translators who can make AI practical for small businesses
- Regulatory trends support AI adoption advisors

## Market Research Insights

### Canadian AI Attitudes (2025 Data)
- **46%** of Canadians believe AI risks outweigh benefits
- **Only 34%** trust AI-generated information
- **39%** excited about AI (vs 60% globally)
- **16%** used generative AI in past year
- **86%** support government AI regulation

### Market Opportunity
- **Low adoption** among Canadian businesses (~12.2%)
- **High skepticism** creates need for trusted local advisors
- **Regulatory compliance** requirements coming (AIDA)
- **First-mover advantage** in Northern BC market
