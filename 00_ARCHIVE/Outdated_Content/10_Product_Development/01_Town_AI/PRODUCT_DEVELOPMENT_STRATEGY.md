## ðŸ“Š Product Development Strategy

### Development Principles
1. **Local First**: Products designed for Northern BC realities
2. **Human-Centered**: AI that augments, not replaces
3. **Transparent**: Open about capabilities and limitations
4. **Sustainable**: Built to last, not trendy
5. **Accessible**: Easy to use and understand

### Development Process
1. **Research Phase**: Market analysis and user research
2. **Design Phase**: Prototype development and testing
3. **Development Phase**: Full product development
4. **Testing Phase**: User acceptance and quality assurance
5. **Launch Phase**: Commercial release and support

### Quality Standards
- **Reliability**: 99.9% uptime for critical systems
- **Security**: Enterprise-grade security and privacy
- **Usability**: Intuitive interfaces and clear documentation
- **Support**: Local support and training
- **Compliance**: Meet all relevant regulations and standards
