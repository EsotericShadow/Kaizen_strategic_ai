- Identify target users and stakeholders

- Establish success metrics and timelines

## üîÑ What Still Applies to Kaizen Strategic AI

### Core Elements That Transfer
1. **Mission & Vision**: The "move the North forward" philosophy remains central
2. **Customer Segments**: Rabbits, Deer, Elephants segmentation is still valid
3. **Value Propositions**: Human-first technology approach continues
4. **Key Activities**: Discovery and expectation setting processes remain
5. **Customer Relationships**: Trust-based, educational approach persists

### Elements That Evolve
1. **Service Focus**: From web solutions to strategic AI integration
2. **Pricing**: From $95/hour to value-based packages ($2K-$150K+)
3. **Partners**: Addition of AI providers, data center partners
4. **Resources**: Expansion to include AI tools, local data infrastructure
5. **Revenue Streams**: Addition of product licensing, recurring services

### New Elements for Kaizen
1. **AI Product Development**: Town AI, local data center
2. **Physical Products**: Recording devices, smart office hardware
3. **Strategic Consulting**: Digital transformation roadmaps
4. **Training Services**: AI literacy, implementation workshops
5. **Regional Expansion**: Beyond Terrace to Northern BC
