# Northern BC Competitor Database - Kaizen Strategic AI

## Executive Summary
This comprehensive competitor database identifies all potential competitors for Kaizen Strategic AI in Northern British Columbia, categorized by service type, market position, and competitive threat level.

## Competitive Landscape Overview

### Market Characteristics
- **Limited AI Specialists**: Very few dedicated AI consulting firms in Northern BC
- **Emerging Market**: AI adoption still in early stages for most businesses
- **Relationship-Based**: Personal connections and local presence crucial
- **Industry Focus**: Forestry, mining, tourism, and small business sectors dominate

### Competitive Threat Levels
- **HIGH**: Direct competitors offering similar services
- **MEDIUM**: Partial overlap in services or target markets
- **LOW**: Indirect competition or different market segments

## PRIMARY COMPETITORS (HIGH THREAT)

### 1. Meiko Technologies Ltd.
**Location**: Terrace, BC  
**Services**: Custom web development + mobile apps, SPYCE platform  
**Team Size**: 3-5 employees  
**Revenue**: $100K-$500K annually  
**Market Position**: Established incumbent (2+ years operation)  
**Strengths**: 
- Established client base and Chamber relationships
- Dual revenue streams
- Larger team capacity

**Weaknesses**:
- Reputation damage from IP theft incident
- Corporate inertia and slower innovation
- Relationship dilution (clients don't interact with founders)
- Divided focus between multiple business lines

**Competitive Strategy**: David vs Goliath approach - leverage personal charm, innovation agility, and ethical positioning

### 2. Spruce Tree Media
**Location**: Terrace, BC  
**Services**: Web design + marketing, Google Business Profile management  
**Market Position**: Established local competitor  
**Strengths**: 
- Strong local presence

**Weaknesses**:
- Limited to marketing focus
- No AI/automation capabilities
- Reactive rather than strategic approach

**Competitive Strategy**: "They'll run your socials/GBP. I design the entire digital flow and teach your team to own it."

### 3. TLC Solutions
**Location**: Northern BC  
**Services**: MSP/IT services with software automation  
**Market Position**: Established IT services provider  
**Strengths**:

**Competitive Strategy**: Position as strategic AI consultant vs reactive IT support
