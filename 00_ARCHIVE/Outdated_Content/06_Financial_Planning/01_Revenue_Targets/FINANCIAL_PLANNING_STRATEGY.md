## ðŸ’µ Pricing Strategy

### Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based**: Price based on outcomes, not hours
2. **Transparent**: Clear pricing with no hidden fees
3. **Competitive**: Market-competitive but not cheap
4. **Scalable**: Pricing that grows with client success
5. **Northern-Friendly**: Accessible to local businesses

### Service Package Pricing
**Starter Package (Rabbits)**
- **Investment**: $2K-$5K
- **Includes**: Website, basic automation, setup
- **Timeline**: 2-4 weeks
- **ROI**: 3-6 months payback

**Strategy Package (Deer)**
- **Investment**: $6K-$20K
- **Includes**: Full digital strategy, automation, training
- **Timeline**: 4-8 weeks
- **ROI**: 6-12 months payback

**Infrastructure Package (Elephants)**
- **Investment**: $25K-$150K+
- **Includes**: Complete digital transformation, AI systems
- **Timeline**: 3-9 months
- **ROI**: 12-24 months payback

### Pricing Justification
**Cost Structure Analysis**
- **Labor**: $100-$150/hour (market rate)
- **Overhead**: 20% (insurance, tools, etc.)
- **Profit Margin**: 30-40%
- **Total Rate**: $150-$250/hour

**Value Proposition**
- **Time Savings**: 10-20 hours/month per client
- **Efficiency Gains**: 15-30% improvement
- **Revenue Growth**: 10-25% increase
- **Cost Reduction**: 20-40% operational savings
