## Key Business Plan Highlights

### Company Identity
**Kaizen Strategic AI** - AI-first Digital Strategy firm focused on amplifying human potential through practical technology solutions.

### Mission
"Moving the North forward through Digital Strategy" - Helping organizations evolve through intelligent systems that amplify human ability rather than replace it.

### Target Market
Primary focus on growing businesses (5-50 employees) in Northern BC's northwest region (Terrace, Kitimat, Prince Rupert, Smithers).

### Revenue Strategy
- **Year 1**: $100K-$150K (realistic case scenario)
- **Year 2**: $200K-$300K
- **Year 3**: $400K-$600K
- **Revenue Mix**: 70% project-based, 30% recurring

### Competitive Advantages
- First-mover advantage in Northern BC AI market
- Personal service from solo founder
- Local expertise and understanding
- Innovation leadership with proven reliability
- Educational approach (teaching before selling)

### Pioneer Advantage
- **Market Position**: Not competing, creating the category
- **Former Competitors as Clients**: Local agencies now need our AI expertise
- **Trade Definition**: Pioneering the "AI Technician" profession
- **Regional Authority**: Building Northern BC as the AI hub
- **Awareness Strategy**: Currently 5% market awareness, building to regional recognition

### Growth Strategy
- **Phase 1**: Foundation building and local market establishment
- **Phase 2**: Market expansion and team building
- **Phase 3**: Market leadership and regional expansion

### Success Metrics
- **Client Satisfaction**: 95%+ satisfaction rating
- **Market Share**: 25% of Northern BC AI market by year 2
- **Revenue Growth**: 20-30% monthly growth
- **Referral Rate**: 40% of new clients from referrals
