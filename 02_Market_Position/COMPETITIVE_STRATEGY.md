# Market Position & Competitive Strategy

## Market Analysis
### Geographic Focus
**Primary Market**: Northern BC Northwest Region
- Terrace, BC (home base)
- Kitimat, BC (industrial focus)
- Prince Rupert, BC (port city)
- Smithers, BC (agricultural/tourism)

**Market Characteristics**:
- Population: ~50,000 across target region
- Key Industries: Manufacturing, forestry, mining, tourism, professional services
- Digital Engagement: High online presence, mobile-first
- Business Culture: Relationship-based, local preference

### Target Market Profile
**Primary**: Growing businesses (5-50 employees) with computer/admin processes
**Characteristics**:
- Established businesses with growth potential
- Already online but systems are disconnected
- Have internal processes but need optimization
- Value strategic thinking and long-term planning
- Willing to invest in proper solutions

**Pain Points**:
- Juggling multiple disconnected tools
- Staff confusion about workflows
- Manual processes eating up time
- Lack of clear digital roadmap
- Website doesn't integrate with operations
