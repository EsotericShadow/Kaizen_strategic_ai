# Kaizen Strategic AI - Company Overview

## Company Identity
**Name**: Kaizen Strategic AI  
**Founder**: Gabriel Lacroix  
**Location**: Terrace, BC (Northern BC)  
**Founded**: 2025  
**Status**: Solo founder operation  

## Mission Statement
**"Moving the North forward through Digital Strategy"**

We help organizations evolve through intelligent systems that amplify human ability rather than replace it. We believe technology should enhance human potential, not diminish it.

## Vision Statement
**"A North where every business feels at home in the digital world"**

We see a North where every business feels confident in their systems, clear on their direction, and proud of what they've built. Our vision is simple: make technology feel human again.

## Value Proposition
**"We make technology make sense"**

Kaizen Strategic AI makes technology practical, ethical, and accessible so every business, from local to global, can work smarter.

## Core Philosophy
- **Human-First**: Technology amplifies human potential, doesn't replace it
- **Continuous Improvement**: Kaizen philosophy of incremental progress
- **Practical Solutions**: Real tools that work, not trendy buzzwords
- **Local Focus**: Built for Northern BC, understanding local realities
- **Trust-Based**: Long-term partnerships over transactional relationships

## Brand Positioning
**"AI-first Digital Strategy that amplifies human potential"**

We provide Digital Strategy services that combine AI, traditional programming, and automation to solve business problems. We refine existing systems before integrating new solutions, making technology easy to get started with and delivering measurable time savings.

## Target Market
**Primary**: Growing businesses (5-50 employees) in Northern BC's northwest region  
**Secondary**: Any business with computer/admin processes that take too long  
**Geographic Focus**: Terrace, Kitimat, Prince Rupert, Smithers

## Competitive Advantages
- **First-Mover**: Early AI adoption in Northern BC market
- **Personal Service**: Solo founder providing direct access
- **Local Expertise**: Understanding of Northern BC business realities
- **Innovation Leadership**: Cutting-edge solutions with proven reliability
- **Educational Approach**: Teaching before selling

## Pioneer Advantage
**Market Position**: First-mover advantage in Northern BC AI consulting - we're not competing, we're creating the category.

**Former Competitors as Clients**: Local web agencies, IT providers, and marketing firms now need our AI expertise - we've moved upstream from service provider to strategic partner.

**Trade Definition**: We're pioneering the "AI Technician" profession - hands-on implementers who wire intelligence through businesses, similar to how electricians wire power through buildings.

**Regional Authority**: Building Northern BC as the AI hub, training the next generation of AI technicians and establishing Kaizen as the regional standard.

**Awareness Strategy**: Currently at 5% market awareness - building from foundation stage to regional recognition through consistent education and demonstration.

## Business Model
- **Service Revenue**: Digital Strategy consulting and implementation
- **Pricing**: Package-based + hourly options
- **Revenue Mix**: 70% project-based, 30% recurring
- **Growth Strategy**: Referral-based + educational marketing
