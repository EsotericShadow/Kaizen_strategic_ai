# Coast Mountain College Outreach Strategy

**Organization**: Coast Mountain College (CMTN)  
**Priority**: STRATEGIC - Tier 2  
**Timeline**: Week 5-6

---

## Strategic Objective

Establish partnership with CMTN to:
1. Host AI governance workshops
2. Develop training programs
3. Establish NCI as community education resource
4. Gain educational institution endorsement

---

## Approach

### Phase 1: Initial Contact (Week 5)
**Contact**: President or Dean of Continuing Education

**Key Messages**:
- Educational partnership opportunity
- Workshop hosting for community
- Training program development
- Local collaboration (both Terrace-based)

**Tone**: Collaborative, community-focused, educational

### Phase 2: Partnership Development (Week 6-8)
**If Interest Expressed**:
- Workshop proposal
- Training curriculum outline
- Partnership agreement discussion
