# PacifiCan RAII Funding Strategy

**Organization**: Pacific Economic Development Canada  
**Program**: Regional AI Initiative (RAII)  
**Priority**: STRATEGIC - Tier 2  
**Timeline**: Week 5-6 (After initial partnerships established)

## Strategic Objective

Secure RAII funding to support:
1. Standards development (NC-AI-002, NC-AI-006)
2. Training programs and workshops
3. Annual AI & Technology Summit
4. Partnership development
5. Regional outreach and adoption

## Key Messaging

### NCI as Eligible Recipient
- **Non-profit**: NCI is non-profit (once incorporated)
- **Regional Focus**: Northern BC (remote/northern area)
- **Ecosystem Building**: Creating standards, training, partnerships
- **Focus Areas**: Health (AI in healthcare), Environment (resource sector), Economic Resilience (small org support)

### What Funding Would Support
1. **Standards Development**: NC-AI-002, NC-AI-006
2. **Training Programs**: Workshops for Northern BC organizations
3. **Summit**: Annual AI & Technology Summit
4. **Partnerships**: Indigenous, academic, business partnerships
5. **Outreach**: Regional adoption and awareness

- Regional AI ecosystem builder for Northern BC

- Serving remote/northern underserved areas

### Expected Outcomes
- Standards adoption across Northern BC
- Training programs delivered
- Partnerships established
- Regional AI governance capacity built
