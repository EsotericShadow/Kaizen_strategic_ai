# Indigenous Bands Visit Plan

**Status**: Planning Phase  
**Created**: 2025-11-02  
**Approach**: Proactive visit with advance notice and information package

---

## Strategy Overview

**Approach**: 
1. Email all band offices in advance
2. Inform them of planned visit date
3. Include comprehensive information package
4. Request meeting/availability
5. Visit in person (respectful, collaborative)

**Tone**: Respectful, collaborative, acknowledging sovereignty, partnership-focused

---

## Visit Schedule Planning

### Suggested Timeline
- **Week 1**: Send emails with visit dates
- **Week 2-3**: Follow-up calls
- **Week 4-5**: Visit dates (spread across multiple days)
- **Week 6**: Follow-up after visits

### Geographic Clustering
Plan visits by geographic clusters to minimize travel:

**Cluster 1: Terrace Area** (Day 1)
- Kitselas First Nation
- Kitsumkalum First Nation
- Tsimshian First Nations

**Cluster 2: Kitimat Area** (Day 2)
- Haisla Nation
- Gitga'at First Nation

**Cluster 3: Prince Rupert Area** (Day 3)
- Lax Kw'alaams Band
- Metlakatla First Nation

**Cluster 4: Smithers Area** (Day 4)
- Gitxsan Nation
- Wet'suwet'en First Nation

**Cluster 5: Regional** (Day 5+)
- Nisga'a Nation
- Tahltan Nation
- Others as identified

## Information Package Contents

### Email Package Should Include:

1. **Cover Letter**: Introduction and purpose
2. **About NCI**: Who we are, what we do
3. **NC-AI-001 Template**: What we're offering (free)
4. **Partnership Opportunity**: What we're seeking
5. **Indigenous Data Sovereignty**: Our commitment
6. **Visit Details**: Date, time, purpose
7. **Meeting Request**: What we'd like to discuss
