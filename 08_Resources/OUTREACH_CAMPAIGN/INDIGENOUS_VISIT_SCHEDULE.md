# Indigenous Bands Visit Schedule

**Status**: Planning Phase  
**Created**: 2025-11-02  
**Purpose**: Coordinate visits to First Nations band offices

---

## Visit Schedule Planning

### Timing Considerations
- **Advance Notice**: 2-3 weeks minimum
- **Band Office Hours**: Typically Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM
- **Council Meetings**: May be scheduled (avoid conflicts)
- **Cultural Events**: Respect traditional calendar
- **Seasonal Considerations**: Winter travel may be challenging

## Visit Date Selection

### Recommended Approach
1. **Email All Bands**: Send email package 3 weeks before preferred visit period
2. **Ask for Availability**: Let bands suggest dates that work for them
3. **Coordinate Clusters**: Group visits by geography to minimize travel
4. **Be Flexible**: Adjust schedule based on band availability

### Example Email Timing
**If you want to visit in Week of [DATE]**:
- **3 Weeks Before**: Send initial emails
- **2 Weeks Before**: Follow-up calls, confirm dates
- **1 Week Before**: Final confirmations, prepare materials
- **Visit Week**: Conduct visits

## Travel Planning

### Distances (from Terrace)
- **Terrace → Kitimat**: ~60 km (1 hour)
- **Terrace → Prince Rupert**: ~150 km (2 hours)
- **Terrace → Smithers**: ~350 km (4 hours)
- **Terrace → Hazelton**: ~380 km (4.5 hours)
- **Terrace → Nass Valley**: ~400 km (5+ hours)

### Accommodation
- **Terrace**: Home base
- **Kitimat**: Day trip or overnight
- **Prince Rupert**: Overnight recommended
- **Smithers/Hazelton**: Overnight recommended
- **Nass Valley**: Overnight required

### Weather Considerations
- **Winter**: Snow, road conditions may delay travel
- **Spring/Fall**: Best travel conditions
- **Summer**: Good conditions, but may conflict with cultural events

## Visit Preparation Checklist

### Before Visits (1 Week Prior)
- [ ] All emails sent with visit dates
- [ ] All confirmations received
- [ ] Travel routes planned
- [ ] Accommodation booked (if needed)
- [ ] Materials prepared (printed copies)
- [ ] Visit agenda prepared
- [ ] Questions prepared
- [ ] Notebook/business cards ready

### During Visits
- [ ] Arrive 10 minutes early
- [ ] Bring printed materials
- [ ] Listen actively
- [ ] Take notes
- [ ] Respect protocols
- [ ] Ask permission for photos (if any)
- [ ] Follow their lead

### After Visits
- [ ] Send thank you email within 24 hours
- [ ] Document feedback
- [ ] Follow up on action items
- [ ] Update contact database

---

## Alternative: Virtual Visits

### If In-Person Not Possible
- **Video Call**: Zoom, Teams, or preferred platform
- **Same Package**: Send email package in advance
- **Same Agenda**: Follow same discussion structure
- **Flexibility**: Some bands may prefer virtual

**Note**: In-person preferred for relationship building, but virtual is respectful alternative if bands prefer.
