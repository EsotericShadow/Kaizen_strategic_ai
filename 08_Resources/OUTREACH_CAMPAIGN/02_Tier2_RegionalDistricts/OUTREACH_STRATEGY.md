# Regional Districts Outreach Strategy

**Type**: Government - Regional Districts  
**Targets**: Bulkley-Nechako, Kitimat-Stikine, Skeena-Queen Charlotte  
**Priority**: STRATEGIC - Tier 2  
**Timeline**: Week 5-6

---

## Strategic Objective

Get regional districts to adopt or endorse NC-AI-001, creating:
1. Early government adopters
2. Case studies and testimonials
3. Regional credibility
4. Influence on member municipalities

---

## Approach

### Priority Order
1. **Bulkley-Nechako** (has AI policy - immediate need)
2. **Kitimat-Stikine** (Terrace area - local priority)
3. **Skeena-Queen Charlotte** (Prince Rupert area)

### Phase 1: Bulkley-Nechako (Week 5)
**Why First**: Already has AI staff policy (Sep 2025) - needs implementation tools

**Approach**:
- Email CAO positioning NC-AI-001 as implementation tool
- Offer to complement existing policy
- Request feedback on template
- Offer presentation to board (if appropriate)

### Phase 2: Kitimat-Stikine & Skeena-Queen Charlotte (Week 6)
**Approach**:
- Email CAO with proactive AI governance offer
- Position as planning tool before AI adoption
- Offer workshops and support

---

## Key Messaging

### For Bulkley-Nechako (Has Policy)
- "NC-AI-001 complements your existing AI policy"
