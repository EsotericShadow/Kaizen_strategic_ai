## Follow-Up Sequence

### If No Response (1 Week After Email)
**Follow-Up Email**:
- Subject: Re: Partnership Opportunity - [Band Name]
- Message: Respectful follow-up, offer alternative dates, leave door open

**Follow-Up Phone Call**:
- Call band office
- Ask for appropriate contact person
- Briefly explain purpose
- Request meeting

### If Positive Response
**Confirmation Email**:
- Thank you
- Confirm date, time, location
- List what you'll bring
- Ask if they need anything

- [ ] Bring printed materials

- [ ] Listen actively
