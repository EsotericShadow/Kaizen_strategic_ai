# Email Sending Checklist for Indigenous Bands

**Purpose**: Ensure all emails are properly customized before sending  
**Timing**: Send 2-3 weeks before visit dates

---

## Pre-Send Checklist (For Each Band Email)

### Research
- [ ] Band name verified (correct spelling)
- [ ] Chief name found and verified
- [ ] Administrator name found (if applicable)
- [ ] Band office email verified (try multiple formats)
- [ ] Band office phone number found
- [ ] Website checked for current information
- [ ] Recent initiatives or news (if any)

### Email Customization
- [ ] Visit date customized for this specific band
- [ ] Visit time suggested (with flexibility noted)
- [ ] Greeting personalized with Chief/Administrator name
- [ ] Band name mentioned correctly throughout
- [ ] Geographic references appropriate (Terrace area, etc.)
- [ ] Tone is respectful and collaborative
- [ ] No generic placeholders remaining

### Attachments
- [ ] About NCI (1-page summary) - attached
- [ ] NC-AI-001-Complete-Template.pdf - attached
- [ ] Partnership Proposal - attached
- [ ] Indigenous Data Sovereignty Commitment - attached
- [ ] All attachments are PDF format (or appropriate format)
- [ ] All attachments are readable and professional

### Review
- [ ] Email length is appropriate (not too long)
- [ ] Clear call to action (request for visit confirmation)
- [ ] Contact information is correct
- [ ] No typos or errors
- [ ] Professional tone maintained
- [ ] Respectful throughout

## Important Notes

### Flexibility
- Be flexible with visit dates - bands may prefer different dates
- Adjust schedule based on band availability
- Some bands may prefer virtual meetings
- Respect band office schedules and protocols

### Respect
- Always acknowledge sovereignty
- Follow band office procedures
- Respect traditional protocols
- Ask permission before visiting
- Listen more than talk

### Patience
- Band offices receive many inquiries
- Response may take time
- Follow up respectfully
- Don't pressure or rush
