# Individual Band Email Templates

**Purpose**: Customized emails for each First Nations band with specific visit dates  
**Status**: Ready for Customization  
**Timing**: Send 2-3 weeks before visit dates

---

## Email Structure

Each band gets:
1. **Customized Email**: Personalized with band name, Chief name, visit date
2. **Information Package**: 4 attachments (About NCI, NC-AI-001, Partnership Proposal, Data Sovereignty Commitment)
3. **Visit Date**: Specific date for that band
4. **Follow-Up**: Phone call 1 week before visit
