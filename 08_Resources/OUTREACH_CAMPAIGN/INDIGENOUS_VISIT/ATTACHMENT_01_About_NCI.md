# About Northern Cascadia Institute of AI Governance

**One-Page Summary for Indigenous Bands Visit**

---

## Who We Are

**Northern Cascadia Institute of AI Governance (NCI)** is a non-profit organization developing regional AI governance standards specifically for Northern BC (north of Prince George).

**Mission**: Provide practical, accessible AI governance tools designed for Northern BC's unique contexts:
- Resource-based economies
- Remote/low-density communities  
- Small organizations (5-50 employees)
- Indigenous sovereignty and data rights

---

## What We Do

**Develop Regional Standards**: 
- NC-AI-001: AI Risk Register Template (published, free)
- NC-AI-002: Implementation Guide (planned)
- NC-AI-006: Indigenous Data Sovereignty Protocol (planned with Indigenous partners)

**Provide Free Tools**:
- All standards are public domain (free to use and adapt)
- Multiple formats (PDF, Word, CSV, Markdown)
- ISO 42001 aligned (works with international standards)

**Build Partnerships**:
- Indigenous organizations (co-development)
- Academic institutions (validation)
- Government (regional collaboration)
- Businesses (practical implementation)

---

## Regional Focus

**Geographic Scope**: Northern BC (north of Prince George)
- Terrace, Kitimat, Prince Rupert, Smithers
- Resource sectors (mining, forestry, energy)
- Remote communities
- Indigenous territories

**Why Regional?**: Generic frameworks don't address:
- Small organization needs
- Remote community challenges
- Resource economy contexts
- Indigenous data sovereignty

---

## Indigenous Data Sovereignty Commitment

**Core Principle**: Respect for Indigenous sovereignty and data rights

**Our Approach**:
- **Partnership, Not Direction**: We collaborate, we don't dictate
- **OCAP® Alignment**: Our standards align with Ownership, Control, Access, Possession principles
- **Indigenous-Led Development**: Co-develop standards with Indigenous partners
- **Traditional Knowledge Protection**: Respect cultural knowledge
- **Data Sovereignty**: Support Indigenous control over data

**Current Work**:
- NC-AI-001 includes Indigenous considerations
- Planning NC-AI-006 (Indigenous Data Sovereignty Protocol) with First Nations Technology Council
- Seeking Indigenous input on all standards

---

## NC-AI-001: AI Risk Register Template

**What It Is**: Free template to assess AI risks in your organization

**Includes**:
- 28 pre-populated AI risks across 8 categories
- Risk scoring matrix (impact × likelihood)
- Control strategies and mitigation
- ISO 42001 aligned

**Formats Available**:
- PDF (download)
- Word (.docx)
- Markdown (.md)
- CSV (for Excel)

**Cost**: Free (public domain)

**Download**: kaizenstrategic.ai/NCI-AI-Governance
