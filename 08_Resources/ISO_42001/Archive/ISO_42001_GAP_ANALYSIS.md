This gap analysis identifies what Kaizen Strategic AI needs to implement to achieve ISO 42001:2023 certification. It compares our current state against ISO 42001 requirements.

## Risk of Not Implementing

### Business Risks
- **Competitive Disadvantage**: Competitors may get certified first
- **Lost Opportunities**: Clients may prefer certified providers
- **Regulatory Risk**: Future AI regulations may require certification
- **Reputation Risk**: Lack of formal AI governance may concern clients
- **Liability Risk**: Less protection against AI-related claims

### Client Concerns
- **Trust**: Clients may question AI governance
- **Assurance**: No third-party validation of AI practices
- **Compliance**: Clients may need certified partners
- **Differentiation**: Harder to stand out from competition
