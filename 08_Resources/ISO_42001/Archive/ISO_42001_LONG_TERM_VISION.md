## Bundle vs. Buying Separately Over Time

### The Smart Strategy

**Don't buy bundles until you need them.**

### Why:
- Standards change over time
- You don't know what you'll need 3 years from now
- Better to buy when requirement is clear
- No point paying for standards you never use

### Example Timeline:

**Year 1** (Now):
- Buy ISO 42001 alone ($280 CAD)

**Year 2** (Data center launch):
- Buy ISO 27001 alone ($300 CAD)
- Buy ISO 27017 ($200 CAD)
- Buy ISO 27018 ($200 CAD)
- **Total**: ~$700 CAD

**Year 3** (Infrastructure provider):
- Add ISO 22301, ISO 20000, etc. as needed
- **Cost**: Varies by what you actually need

**Total over 3 years**: ~$1,000-$2,000 CAD  
**Buying bundles**: Would cost ~$1,500+ CAD  
**Savings**: Buy only what you need, when you need it

## Data Center Infrastructure Standards Deep Dive

### ISO 27001 - Information Security ✅ MUST HAVE
**Why**: Industry standard for data centers  
**Covers**: Info security, data protection, access controls  
**Timeline**: 6-12 months to certify  
**Cost**: $5K-$15K audit + $300 standard  

**Without it**: Enterprise clients won't trust your infrastructure

---

### ISO 27017 - Cloud Security ✅ MUST HAVE
**Why**: Cloud-specific security controls  
**Covers**: Cloud computing security, shared responsibility  
**Timeline**: Often certified alongside 27001  
**Cost**: Often combined audit with 27001  

**Without it**: Cloud hosting without security assurance

---

### ISO 27018 - Cloud Privacy ✅ HIGHLY RECOMMENDED
**Why**: Privacy in cloud services  
**Covers**: PII in cloud, privacy controls  
**Timeline**: Often certified alongside 27001  
**Cost**: Often combined audit with 27001  

**Without it**: Privacy-conscious clients may choose elsewhere

---

### ISO 22301 - Business Continuity ⚠️ NICE TO HAVE
**Why**: 24/7 operations require continuity  
**Covers**: Disaster recovery, business continuity planning  
**Timeline**: 6-12 months to certify  
**Cost**: Separate certification audit  

**Without it**: Works fine, but less enterprise credibility

---

### ISO 20000 - IT Service Management ⚠️ NICE TO HAVE
**Why**: Managed services need service management  
**Covers**: Service management, SLA management  
**Timeline**: 6-12 months to certify  
**Cost**: Separate certification audit  

**Without it**: You can still offer services, just less structured
