## The Big Picture

**ISO 42001:2023** is the first international standard for AI Management Systems. Getting certified demonstrates:
- Systematic approach to responsible AI
- Trust and credibility with clients
- Competitive differentiation
- Regulatory readiness

**Certification Timeline**: 6-8 months  
**Estimated Cost**: $20K-$40K (initial), $2K-$5K/year (maintenance)  
**Estimated Effort**: 150-210 hours

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I do this without a consultant?**  
A: Yes, but it will take longer and be more challenging. A consultant can save time and reduce risk of mistakes.

**Q: How long does certification last?**  
A: 3 years, with annual surveillance audits. Full re-certification every 3 years.

**Q: What happens if I fail an audit?**  
A: You get nonconformities to address. Critical findings must be fixed before certification. Minor findings can be fixed later.

**Q: Do I need all procedures from day 1?**  
A: No, but you need them implemented before certification audit. Start with high-priority procedures.

**Q: Can I become certified if I'm a solo founder?**  
A: Yes! Many small businesses achieve ISO certification. Solo founders have clear accountability advantage.
