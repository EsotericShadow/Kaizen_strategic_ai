# Insurance Requirements for ISO 42001 & Small Business

## Quick Answer

**ISO 42001 doesn't require specific insurance**, but you still need business insurance. Here's what you need and what it costs:

---

## What Insurance Do You Need?

### Required for ISO 42001: None ✅
ISO 42001 doesn't mandate any insurance. The standard focuses on AI governance and risk management, not insurance requirements.

### What You Actually Need (Business Basics):

✅ **Professional Liability (E&O)**: $1M+ coverage  
✅ **Cyber Liability**: Data breach protection  
✅ **Commercial General Liability (CGL)**: $2M+ coverage  
⚠️ **Business Equipment**: Computers, software (optional)  
⚠️ **Directors & Officers**: Decision-making coverage (optional for solo founder)

---

## Annual Insurance Costs for Small Solo Business

### Professional Liability (E&O) - ✅ REQUIRED
**Coverage**: $1M - $2M  
**What it covers**: 
- Negligence claims
- AI system failures
- Bad advice or recommendations
- Project errors

**Annual Cost**: **$1,500 - $3,000 CAD**
- Solo consultant: ~$1,500-$2,000
- Low-risk projects: Lower end
- High-risk projects: Upper end

---

### Cyber Liability - ✅ REQUIRED FOR AI BUSINESS
**Coverage**: $250K - $1M  
**What it covers**:
- Data breaches
- Hacking incidents
- Client data loss
- Breach notification costs

**Annual Cost**: **$1,500 - $2,500 CAD**
- Small business average: ~$1,750
- AI/data handling: Higher end
- With ISO 42001: May get discount

---

### Commercial General Liability - ✅ REQUIRED
**Coverage**: $2M  
**What it covers**:
- Bodily injury
- Property damage
- Advertising injury
- General business operations

**Annual Cost**: **$400 - $800 CAD**
- Solo consultant: ~$500-$600
- Home office: Lower end
- Client visits: Middle range

---

### Business Equipment - ⚠️ OPTIONAL
**Coverage**: Computers, software, office equipment  
**Annual Cost**: **$200 - $500 CAD**

**Skip if**: Equipment is old or you can afford to replace it

---

### Directors & Officers - ⚠️ OPTIONAL FOR SOLO
**Coverage**: Decision-making liability  
**Annual Cost**: **$600 - $1,200 CAD**

**Skip if**: Solo founder, no board, no significant corporate decisions

---

## Total Annual Insurance Costs

### Minimum (Required Only):
- Professional Liability: $1,500

## Tips to Reduce Insurance Costs

### Discounts Available:

✅ **ISO 42001 Certification**: 10-20% off cyber liability  
✅ **Bundled Policies**: Combine multiple coverages (5-15% off)  
✅ **Paid Annually**: vs. monthly (save ~5%)  
✅ **Higher Deductibles**: Lower premiums (if you can afford it)  
✅ **Good Claims History**: No claims = lower rates  
✅ **Security Measures**: VPN, encryption, backups documented  
✅ **Home-Based Business**: Lower CGL costs  

### The ISO 42001 Bonus:
Having ISO 42001 can save you **$200-$400/year** on insurance while also giving you better coverage terms.

## Common Exclusions to Watch For

### What Might NOT Be Covered:

⚠️ **AI-Specific Claims**: Some policies exclude AI failures  
⚠️ **Data Training**: Using client data to train AI  
⚠️ **Third-Party Tools**: Failures of ChatGPT, Claude, etc.  
⚠️ **Known Vulnerabilities**: Not fixing known security issues  
⚠️ **Intentional Acts**: Deliberate wrongdoing  

### With ISO 42001:
- More likely to get AI coverage
- Better protection overall
- Fewer exclusions

**Action**: Get certificates of insurance from third-party AI vendors (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.)
