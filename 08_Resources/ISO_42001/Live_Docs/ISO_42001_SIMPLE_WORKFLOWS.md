## DECISION TREES

### Decision Tree: Is This Risky?

```
START: New AI use case
    ‚Üì
Uses personal data?
    ‚Üì YES ‚Üí MEDIUM RISK
    ‚Üì NO  ‚Üí Continue
    ‚Üì
Makes important business decisions?
    ‚Üì YES ‚Üí MEDIUM RISK
    ‚Üì NO  ‚Üí Continue
    ‚Üì
Could cause financial loss if wrong?
    ‚Üì YES ‚Üí HIGH RISK
    ‚Üì NO  ‚Üí Continue
    ‚Üì
Could harm people if wrong?
    ‚Üì YES ‚Üí CRITICAL RISK
    ‚Üì NO  ‚Üí LOW RISK
```

**Actions by Risk Level:**
- **LOW**: Use it, document it
- **MEDIUM**: Human review, extra testing
- **HIGH**: Impact assessment, safeguards, client sign-off
- **CRITICAL**: Senior review, extensive testing, backup plans
