## Publication Formats

### White Papers
- **Format**: PDF + web HTML
- **Length**: 6-8 pages (full), 2-3 pages (executive summary)
- **Style**: Professional, evidence-based, citable
- **Design**: Professional layout, charts/graphics

### Articles
- **Format**: Web + PDF (for submissions)
- **Length**: 500-1200 words (depending on publication)
- **Style**: Accessible, practical, engaging
- **Distribution**: Newspapers, business publications, website

### Case Studies
- **Format**: Web + PDF
- **Length**: 1-2 pages
- **Style**: Visual, practical, testimonial-focused
- **Design**: Infographics, quotes, metrics

### Annual Reports
- **Format**: PDF + web HTML
- **Length**: 10-15 pages
- **Style**: Comprehensive, data-driven, professional
- **Design**: Charts, maps, infographics

### Blog Series
- **Format**: Web (blog platform)
- **Length**: 500-1000 words per post
- **Frequency**: Monthly or bi-weekly
- **Style**: Educational, practical, accessible
