# Kaizen Strategic AI - Legal Compliance Checklist

## Pre-Launch Legal Requirements

### Business Structure & Registration
- [ ] **Incorporation**: BC Corporation registered and active
- [ ] **Business Name**: "Kaizen Strategic AI" registered and protected
- [ ] **Business License**: Local municipality business license obtained
- [ ] **GST/HST Registration**: Registered if revenue exceeds $30K threshold
- [ ] **Corporate Records**: Minute book, bylaws, and resolutions maintained

### Insurance Coverage
- [ ] **Professional Liability (E&O)**: $1M+ coverage for consulting services
- [ ] **Cyber Liability**: Coverage for data breaches and cyber incidents
- [ ] **Commercial General Liability**: $2M+ coverage for general business operations
- [ ] **Business Equipment**: Coverage for computers, software, and office equipment
- [ ] **Directors & Officers**: Coverage for corporate decision-making

### Contract Templates & Legal Documents
- [ ] **Terms & Conditions**: Comprehensive terms covering all services
- [ ] **Contract Templates**: Different templates for various service types
- [ ] **Privacy Policy**: PIPEDA/PIPA compliant privacy policy
- [ ] **Data Processing Agreements**: For third-party AI tool usage
- [ ] **Non-Disclosure Agreements**: For confidential client information

### Intellectual Property Protection
- [ ] **Trademark**: "Kaizen Strategic AI" trademark application filed
- [ ] **Copyright**: Documentation and methodologies protected
- [ ] **Trade Secrets**: Proprietary methods and frameworks documented
- [ ] **Client IP**: Clear ownership clauses in all contracts
- [ ] **Open Source**: Compliance with any open source licenses used

### Data Protection & Privacy Compliance
- [ ] **PIPEDA Compliance**: Personal information protection procedures
- [ ] **BC PIPA Compliance**: Provincial privacy act compliance
- [ ] **Data Retention**: Policies for data storage and deletion
- [ ] **Consent Management**: Procedures for obtaining client consent
- [ ] **Breach Response**: Incident response procedures documented

### AI-Specific Compliance
- [ ] **AI Governance Policy**: Internal AI ethics and governance framework
- [ ] **Bias Assessment**: Procedures for identifying and mitigating AI bias
- [ ] **Transparency Requirements**: Documentation of AI decision-making processes
- [ ] **Human Oversight**: Procedures for human review of AI outputs
- [ ] **Third-Party AI Tools**: Compliance with vendor terms and conditions

### Employment & Contractor Agreements
- [ ] **Employment Contracts**: For any future employees
- [ ] **Independent Contractor Agreements**: For freelancers and consultants
- [ ] **Non-Compete Clauses**: Appropriate restrictions for key personnel
- [ ] **Confidentiality Agreements**: For all team members and contractors
- [ ] **IP Assignment**: Clear ownership of work product

### Financial & Tax Compliance
- [ ] **Bookkeeping System**: Proper financial record keeping
- [ ] **Tax Filings**: Corporate tax returns and GST/HST returns
- [ ] **Payroll System**: If hiring employees
- [ ] **Expense Tracking**: Business expense documentation
- [ ] **Audit Trail**: Complete financial audit trail

### Industry-Specific Compliance
- [ ] **Professional Standards**: Adherence to consulting industry standards
- [ ] **Client Confidentiality**: Procedures for protecting client information
- [ ] **Quality Assurance**: Standards for service delivery
- [ ] **Complaint Handling**: Procedures for addressing client concerns
- [ ] **Professional Development**: Ongoing education and certification
