# Kaizen Strategic AI - Discovery Process Framework

## Discovery Process Overview

### Phase 1: Initial Client Assessment
**Duration**: 1-2 hours  
**Objective**: Determine client fit and project scope  
**Deliverable**: Go/No-Go decision and preliminary project outline

### Phase 2: Deep Process Discovery
**Duration**: 4-8 hours  
**Objective**: Map current state and identify optimization opportunities  
**Deliverable**: Comprehensive process analysis and recommendations

### Phase 3: Technical Feasibility Assessment
**Duration**: 2-4 hours  
**Objective**: Evaluate technical requirements and constraints  
**Deliverable**: Technical implementation plan and timeline

### Phase 4: ROI Analysis & Prioritization
**Duration**: 2-3 hours  
**Objective**: Quantify value and prioritize implementation  
**Deliverable**: ROI projections and implementation roadmap

---

## Phase 1: Initial Client Assessment

### Client Qualification Checklist
**Business Profile Assessment**
- [ ] Company size (employees, revenue, industry)
- [ ] Current technology stack and systems
- [ ] Digital maturity level (1-10 scale)
- [ ] Budget range and decision-making process
- [ ] Timeline expectations and constraints
- [ ] Previous AI/automation experience
- [ ] Pain points and inefficiencies
- [ ] Growth plans and scalability needs

**Decision Maker Analysis**
- [ ] Primary decision maker identified
- [ ] Decision-making process and timeline
- [ ] Budget authority and approval process
- [ ] Technical knowledge and comfort level
- [ ] Change management experience
- [ ] Communication preferences
- [ ] Availability for project involvement

**Project Scope Definition**
- [ ] Primary objectives and success metrics
- [ ] Specific processes to be addressed
- [ ] Integration requirements with existing systems
- [ ] Data sources and quality assessment
- [ ] Compliance and security requirements
- [ ] Training and support needs
- [ ] Ongoing maintenance expectations

### Initial Assessment Prompts

**Business Understanding Prompt**:
```
"Based on our conversation, I understand your business is [industry] with [size] employees generating [revenue] annually. Your main pain points are [list 3-5 key issues]. You're looking to [primary objective] within [timeline] with a budget of [range]. 

Let me confirm my understanding and ask a few clarifying questions:
1. What's your current process for [specific pain point]?
2. How much time does this currently take per week?
3. What systems do you currently use for this?
4. What would success look like for you?
5. What's your biggest concern about implementing AI/automation?"
```

**Technical Readiness Assessment Prompt**:
```
"I need to understand your current technology setup to determine the best approach. Can you help me with:

1. What software systems do you currently use? (CRM, accounting, project management, etc.)
2. Do you have any existing automation or integrations?
3. What's your comfort level with new technology? (1-10 scale)
4. Do you have IT support, or would you need us to handle everything?
5. Are there any compliance or security requirements we need to consider?
6. What's your internet speed and reliability like?"
```

## Phase 2: Deep Process Discovery

### Process Mapping Framework

#### Current State Analysis
**Process Identification**
- [ ] List all processes related to the project scope
- [ ] Map process owners and stakeholders
- [ ] Document current workflows and decision points
- [ ] Identify data inputs and outputs
- [ ] Note current tools and systems used
- [ ] Record time estimates for each step
- [ ] Identify bottlenecks and pain points
- [ ] Document error rates and quality issues

**Process Complexity Assessment**
- [ ] Number of steps in current process
- [ ] Number of people involved
- [ ] Number of systems/tools used
- [ ] Frequency of process execution
- [ ] Variability in process execution
- [ ] Decision points and exceptions
- [ ] Integration requirements
- [ ] Compliance and audit requirements

#### Process Optimization Opportunities
**Automation Potential Matrix**

| Process | Current Time | Frequency | Complexity | Automation Potential | Priority |
|---------|--------------|-----------|------------|---------------------|----------|
| [Process 1] | ___ hours | Daily/Weekly/Monthly | Low/Med/High | Low/Med/High | High/Med/Low |
| [Process 2] | ___ hours | Daily/Weekly/Monthly | Low/Med/High | Low/Med/High | High/Med/Low |
| [Process 3] | ___ hours | Daily/Weekly/Monthly | Low/Med/High | Low/Med/High | High/Med/Low |

**Automation Potential Scoring**:
- **High**: 8-10 points (Easy to automate, high impact)
- **Medium**: 5-7 points (Moderate complexity, good impact)
- **Low**: 1-4 points (Complex or low impact)

### Deep Discovery Prompts

**Process Mapping Prompt**:
```
"I need to understand your current [specific process] in detail. Let's walk through it step by step:

1. How does this process currently start? (Trigger/initiation)
2. What's the first thing that happens?
3. Who is involved at each step?
4. What information do they need?
5. What decisions do they make?
6. What tools or systems do they use?
7. How long does each step take?
8. What could go wrong at each step?
9. How do you know when it's complete?
10. What happens with the output?

Can you walk me through a recent example from start to finish?"
```

**Pain Point Analysis Prompt**:
```
"Let's dig deeper into the challenges you're facing:

1. What's the most frustrating part of this process?
2. How often do things go wrong?
3. What's the cost when things go wrong?
4. How much time do you spend fixing problems?
5. What would happen if this process was 50% faster?
6. What would happen if this process was 100% accurate?
7. What's preventing you from improving this process?
8. Have you tried to fix this before? What happened?
9. What would your ideal process look like?
10. What's your biggest fear about changing this process?"
```

### Technical Feasibility Prompts

**System Integration Prompt**:
```
"To determine the best technical approach, I need to understand your current systems:

1. What's your primary business system? (CRM, ERP, etc.)
2. Do you have any existing APIs or integrations?
3. What's your data storage setup? (Cloud, on-premise, hybrid)
4. Do you have any security or compliance requirements?
5. What's your backup and disaster recovery setup?
6. Do you have any IT staff or do you outsource?
7. What's your budget for ongoing software costs?
8. Are you open to cloud-based solutions?
9. Do you need real-time processing or is batch processing okay?
10. What's your comfort level with technical complexity?"
```

**AI Implementation Prompt**:
```
"Let's explore how AI could help with your specific challenges:

1. What type of data do you work with most? (Text, numbers, images, etc.)
2. How much data do you typically process?
3. Do you need the AI to make decisions or just provide recommendations?
4. How important is accuracy vs. speed for your use case?
5. Do you need the AI to learn and improve over time?
6. What's your comfort level with AI making decisions?
7. Do you need to explain AI decisions to others?
8. What's your biggest concern about using AI?
9. Have you used any AI tools before? What was your experience?
10. What would make you feel confident about AI implementation?"
```

## Phase 4: ROI Analysis & Prioritization

### Value Quantification Framework

#### Time Savings Analysis
**Current State Metrics**
- [ ] Time spent on process per week/month
- [ ] Number of people involved
- [ ] Hourly cost of personnel
- [ ] Overtime costs
- [ ] Opportunity cost of time

**Projected Savings**
- [ ] Time reduction percentage
- [ ] Labor cost savings
- [ ] Overtime reduction
- [ ] Opportunity cost recovery
- [ ] Quality improvement benefits

#### Error Reduction Analysis
**Current Error Costs**
- [ ] Error frequency and types
- [ ] Cost per error (time, money, reputation)
- [ ] Customer impact
- [ ] Recovery costs
- [ ] Prevention costs

**Projected Improvements**
- [ ] Error reduction percentage
- [ ] Cost savings from fewer errors
- [ ] Customer satisfaction improvement
- [ ] Reputation protection value
- [ ] Compliance improvement

#### Revenue Impact Analysis
**Growth Opportunities**
- [ ] Capacity for additional clients/customers
- [ ] Faster response times
- [ ] Improved customer experience
- [ ] New service offerings
- [ ] Market expansion potential

**Revenue Projections**
- [ ] Additional revenue from capacity
- [ ] Premium pricing for better service
- [ ] New revenue streams
- [ ] Market share gains
- [ ] Customer retention improvements

### ROI Calculation Framework

**ROI Formula**: (Total Benefits - Total Costs) รท Total Costs ร 100

**Cost Components**:
- Implementation costs
- Software/licensing costs
- Training costs
- Ongoing maintenance costs
- Opportunity costs

**Benefit Components**:
- Time savings (labor costs)
- Error reduction (cost avoidance)
- Revenue increases
- Quality improvements
- Competitive advantages

### ROI Analysis Prompts

**Value Quantification Prompt**:
```
"To calculate the ROI of this project, I need to quantify the current costs and potential savings:

1. How much time do you currently spend on [specific process] per week?
2. What's the hourly cost of the people doing this work?
3. How often do errors occur? What's the cost when they do?
4. How much overtime is required for this process?
5. What opportunities are you missing because of time spent on this?
6. How much could you charge more if this process was faster/better?
7. How many more customers could you serve with the time saved?
8. What's the cost of customer complaints or lost business?
9. How much do you spend on temporary help or contractors?
10. What's the value of your time that could be spent on growth instead?"
```

**Priority Assessment Prompt**:
```
"Let's prioritize which improvements will have the biggest impact:

1. Which process improvement would save you the most time?
2. Which would reduce the most errors?
3. Which would have the biggest impact on customers?
4. Which would be easiest to implement?
5. Which would have the fastest payback?
6. Which would reduce the most stress/frustration?
7. Which would enable the most growth?
8. Which would be most visible to your team?
9. Which would give you the biggest competitive advantage?
10. Which would you be most excited to implement?"
```
