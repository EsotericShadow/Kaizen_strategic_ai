- Assign key personnel for interviews and demonstrations
- Respond to requests for information within 24 hours
- Participate in review meetings as scheduled

### Company Responsibilities

- Provide clear, actionable recommendations
- Deliver all items within agreed timeline
- Maintain confidentiality of all Client information

- Company provides recommendations, not guarantees of specific outcomes
- Client is responsible for validating all recommendations before implementation
- AI tools and outputs are provided "as-is" without warranty

- Payment is due for work completed up to termination date
- Company will return all Client data upon termination
