# Contacts Directory Structure

## Directory Overview
This directory contains all business contacts organized by relationship type and status.

## Contact Categories

### 01_Clients
- **Current Clients**: Active clients with ongoing projects
- **Past Clients**: Previous clients for follow-up/upsell opportunities
- **Retainer Clients**: Monthly/ongoing service clients

### 02_Prospects
- **Hot Prospects**: Qualified leads ready for proposal
- **Warm Prospects**: Engaged leads in discovery phase
- **Cold Prospects**: Initial contact, needs qualification

### 03_Leads
- **Event Leads**: Contacts from workshops, seminars, networking
- **Referral Leads**: Contacts from existing clients/partners
- **Inbound Leads**: Website inquiries, social media contacts
- **Outbound Leads**: Cold outreach contacts

### 04_Key_Contacts
- **Referral Sources**: Chamber members, business partners
- **Industry Influencers**: Local business leaders, thought leaders
- **Media Contacts**: Local journalists, podcast hosts
- **Government Contacts**: Municipal, provincial contacts

### 05_Competition
- **Direct Competitors**: AI consultants, digital agencies
- **Indirect Competitors**: Traditional consultants, freelancers
- **Potential Partners**: Complementary service providers

### 06_Vendors
- **Technology Partners**: AI tool providers, software vendors
- **Service Providers**: Accountants, lawyers, insurance
- **Suppliers**: Equipment, materials, services

## Contact Dossier Template

Each contact should have a dossier containing:
- Contact Information
- Business Context
- Relationship Status
- Project History (if applicable)
- Pain Points & Opportunities
- Budget & Constraints
- Decision Process
- Success Metrics
- Service Level
- Next Steps
- Notes

## Status Tracking

### Client Status
- **Active**: Current projects
- **On Hold**: Paused projects
- **Completed**: Finished projects
- **Upsell Opportunity**: Ready for additional services

### Prospect Status
- **Qualified**: Meets criteria
- **Unqualified**: Doesn't meet criteria
- **Nurturing**: Building relationship
- **Proposal Sent**: Awaiting decision

### Lead Status
- **New**: Just contacted
- **Engaged**: Responding to outreach
- **Qualified**: Meets basic criteria
- **Disqualified**: Not a good fit

## Contact Management Best Practices

1. **Regular Updates**: Update contact information monthly
2. **Status Tracking**: Monitor relationship progression
3. **Follow-up Scheduling**: Set reminders for next contact
4. **Notes Documentation**: Record all interactions
5. **Opportunity Tracking**: Monitor upsell/cross-sell opportunities
